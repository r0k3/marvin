# Bottom's Transformation

## Summary
[[Nick Bottom]] gets a [[donkey]] head.

## Details
While the [[Mechanicals]] are rehearsing their play '[[Pyramus and Thisbe]]' in the [[forest]], [[Puck]] transforms [[Nick Bottom]]'s head into that of a [[donkey]]. [[Titania]], under the [[love potion]]'s spell, wakes up and falls in love with him.
